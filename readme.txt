CAN YOU BE THE WOLF OF WALL STREET? CAN YOU PERFORM THE BIG SHORT?
Challenge your economic interests in this intense unforgiving sotck simulation. Sell too late and money will pass you by, sell to early and you wont make any money at all. You must analyze graph trends, read up on headlines and use a bit of gut luck to become the ultimate stock trader. Work your way up from 1000 dollars to a million, all it takes is skill and hard work. Do you have what it takes?
